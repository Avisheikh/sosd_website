Celebrating Children's Day with Health and Happiness at SOSD

Previous Next

Nestled in the serene mountains of Sindhupalchowk, Nepal, the School of Social Development (SOSD) stands as a beacon of hope and progress for the local community. Founded by the dedicated efforts of Alberto Luzzi, the school provides free education in IT, dance, kindergarten, and grade 1, empowering the indigenous Majhi people. Alberto, a successful entrepreneur turned humanitarian, founded the Jay Nepal NGO in response to the devastating earthquake of April 25, 2015, which left over 900,000 homes destroyed and claimed more than 10,000 lives. His relentless commitment has transformed Bodgaun, turning it from a village of despair into a thriving community.

On May 2, 2024, the School of Social Development and the Bodgaun Medical Center celebrated Children's Day with a special event dedicated to the health and happiness of the local children. The day was marked by a health camp organized at the Bodgaun Medical Center, providing free health check-ups and medicines to children from SOSD and the surrounding community.

This remarkable initiative was made possible by the generous efforts of three dedicated healthcare professionals from Italy: Dr. Emiliano, pharmacist Michele Visini, and Caterina Marchetti. These compassionate volunteers traveled to Bodgaun to offer their expertise and services, ensuring that the children received the best possible care.

Throughout the day, the medical team conducted thorough health examinations, addressing various health concerns and providing essential medications where needed. Their commitment to the well-being of the children was evident in the care and attention they offered to each young patient.

Following the health camp, Dr. Emiliano, Michele Visini, and Caterina Marchetti visited the School of Social Development to join in the Children's Day celebrations. The school was abuzz with excitement as students and staff welcomed their guests with open arms.

Our journey to Bodgaun has been transformative. The dedication of Alberto Luzzi and the Jay Nepal NGO in rebuilding this community is truly inspiring. Every volunteer are committed to supporting the School of Social Development and its mission to provide free education.

The children day began with the ceremonial cutting of cakes, symbolizing the joy and significance of the day. The guests, along with the school staff, distributed slices of cake and cold drinks to the children, creating an atmosphere of joy and children day.

The celebration continued with a captivating cultural dance performance by the students, showcasing the rich heritage and traditions of the Majhi people. The guests were enthralled by the vibrant costumes and lively dances, which added a special touch to the day's events.

Children's Day at the School of Social Development was a perfect blend of health, happiness, and cultural celebration. The free health camp and the joyous festivities underscored the school's commitment to the well-being and holistic development of its students. The visit of Dr. Emiliano, Michele Visini, and Caterina Marchetti not only provided essential healthcare services but also brought a sense of global solidarity and support to the community of Bodgaun.

As SOSD continues to grow and thrive, such events reinforce the importance of nurturing and caring for the youngest members of the community, ensuring they have the health and happiness they deserve.
