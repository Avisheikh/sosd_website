Celebrating Children's Day with Health and Happiness at SOSD

Previous Next

Nestled in the serene mountains of Sindhupalchowk, Nepal, the School of Social Development (SOSD) stands as a beacon of hope and progress for the local community. Founded by the dedicated efforts of Alberto Luzzi, the school provides free education in IT, dance, kindergarten, and grade 1, empowering the indigenous Majhi people. Alberto, a successful entrepreneur turned humanitarian, founded the Jay Nepal NGO in response to the devastating earthquake of April 25, 2015, which left over 900,000 homes destroyed and claimed more than 10,000 lives. His relentless commitment has transformed Bodgaun, turning it from a village of despair into a thriving community.

Our dance program is designed to be inclusive and engaging, catering to students of all skill levels. Whether a beginner or an experienced dancer, each student receives personalized instruction to help them develop their skills and confidence. The classes cover various dance styles, from traditional Nepalese dances that celebrate our rich cultural heritage to contemporary styles that resonate with global trends.

The dance classes are held in a spacious, well-equipped studio, providing an ideal environment for students to learn and practice. Our experienced instructors, passionate about dance and teaching, guide students through each step, ensuring they master the techniques while having fun.

The benefits of dance education extend far beyond the dance floor. Through dance, students gain physical fitness, improve coordination and flexibility, and learn the importance of discipline and perseverance. Dance also enhances cognitive skills, including memory, focus, and creativity.

Moreover, participating in dance classes helps students build self-esteem and fosters a sense of belonging. It provides a positive outlet for self-expression and emotional release, contributing to their overall mental well-being. The collaborative nature of dance encourages teamwork and communication, essential skills for personal and professional success.

Our journey to Bodgaun has been transformative. The dedication of Alberto Luzzi and the Jay Nepal NGO in rebuilding this community is truly inspiring. Every volunteer are committed to supporting the School of Social Development and its mission to provide free education.

The free dance classes at the School of Social Development are a testament to our commitment to empowering the youth of Bodgaun. By providing opportunities for creative expression and personal growth, we are helping students build a brighter future for themselves and their community.

As SOSD continues to grow, we remain dedicated to fostering an environment where every child can explore their potential, express their creativity, and develop the skills needed to succeed in life. Through dance, we celebrate the rich cultural heritage of the Majhi people while embracing the transformative power of art.
